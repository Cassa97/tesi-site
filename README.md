Web platform for browsing and applying to thesis opportunities offered within the research group at **Politecnico di Milano**.

👉 Live website:  
https://cassa97.github.io/tesi-site/

---

## 📌 Overview

This web application allows students to:

- Explore available thesis opportunities
- Filter topics by research area
- Read descriptions, requirements, and methodology
- Apply via pre-filled email
- Propose their own thesis topic

The platform is designed to be lightweight, accessible, and easy to use without authentication.

---

## 🌍 Language Support

The platform supports:

- 🇮🇹 Italian  
- 🇬🇧 English  

Language can be switched using the selector in the top-right corner.

## 🔬 Research Areas
The proposed thesis topics are aligned with the research activities of the group, focusing on the digital transformation of the built environment, with particular attention to interoperability, standardization, and data-driven decision-making.

### 🏗️ Construction and Built Environment
- Digitalization of construction processes  
- Building Information Modeling (BIM)  
- Lifecycle management of buildings and infrastructures  
- Construction management and optimization  

### 🌐 openBIM & Interoperability
- IFC (Industry Foundation Classes) data modeling and validation  
- bSDD (buildingSMART Data Dictionary) integration  
- IDS (Information Delivery Specification) definition and validation  
- Interoperability between BIM tools and platforms  
- Data exchange workflows based on open standards  
- Model checking and compliance verification  

### 🧠 Semantic Web & Knowledge Engineering
- Ontologies and knowledge representation  
- Semantic data integration  
- Linked Building Data  
- Knowledge graphs for engineering systems  

### 🧩 Digital Twins & Information Management
- Semantic Digital Twins  
- Information management for circular construction  
- Data interoperability across lifecycle phases  
- Smart asset and infrastructure management  
- Structured information environments (CDE – Common Data Environment) 

### 📡 IoT, Sensor Systems & Semantic Integration
- Integration of IoT sensor networks in buildings and infrastructures  
- Real-time monitoring and data acquisition (environmental, structural, energy domains)  
- Semantic modeling of sensor data through ontologies and knowledge graphs  
- Linking real-time data streams with BIM and Digital Twin environments  
- Interoperability between IoT platforms and openBIM standards (IFC, IDS, bSDD)  
- Event-driven architectures and data pipelines for smart environments  
- Data fusion, reasoning, and contextualization of heterogeneous sensor data

### 📊 Project Management & Process Control
- Cost estimation and cost control  
- Scheduling and time management (4D BIM)  
- Integration of cost (5D BIM) and performance metrics  
- Risk management and decision support  
- Process modeling and optimization  

### 📜 BIM Standards & Regulatory Frameworks
- BIM standardization (ISO 19650, UNI, buildingSMART standards)  
- Information requirements definition (EIR, AIR, BEP)  
- Digital information management processes  
- Compliance with regulatory frameworks  
- Governance of digital construction processes  

### 🤖 Digital Technologies & AI
- Artificial Intelligence applications in construction and engineering (e.g., Large Language Models, machine learning)  
- Natural Language Processing (NLP) for technical and engineering documentation  
- Automation of workflows and decision support systems  
- AI-driven data extraction, classification, and semantic enrichment  
- Integration of AI with BIM and Digital Twin environments  

### 🔐 Cybersecurity & Systems Reliability
- Security analysis of digital systems  
- Risk assessment in smart infrastructures  
- Secure data exchange in engineering environments  

### 💻 Software & Platform Development
- Web-based platforms for research and education  
- Software engineering for engineering applications  
- User-centered interface design and data visualization
